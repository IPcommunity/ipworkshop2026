Dear IP community,

We are pleased to announce that the Call for Abstracts for the 8th International Induced Polarization Workshop is open. Conference registration and abstract submission are handled through a single Google Form, which is available here on the conference website homepage.  

Participants may register with or without submitting an abstract.

**The abstract submission deadline is February 28, 2026**. After this date, the registration form will remain open, but abstract uploads will be disabled. Notification of acceptance will be sent to submitting authors on March 13, 2026. 

We encourage you to visit the conference website for full details and to circulate this announcement within your networks.

We look forward to your participation and contributions.
