We are pleased to announce that there will be no registration fees for the upcoming IP Workshop in Canada. 

From the start, one of our objectives was to keep the workshop affordable and accessible, particularly for students. Thanks to the support of our sponsors and Polytechnique Montréal, anyone may register and attend the workshop at no cost. 

Participants remain responsible for their own travel and lodging (which includes restaurant meals) at the Auberge.

The workshop registration form remains open. Although there are no registration fees, all participants must complete the registration form available on the workshop website, including those who did not submit an abstract: https://internationalipworkshop.com/ 

April 6 is the deadline to reserve a room or condo at the Auberge, after which availability cannot be guaranteed. Please submit your reservation forms, also available on the website, directly to the Auberge. 

The scientific committee is currently reviewing 43 submitted abstracts, most of which are extended abstracts with a preference for oral presentation. Acceptance decisions will be communicated by March 13. If you or members of your team were unable to submit an abstract earlier but would still like to contribute, please contact me directly. We can accept late submissions until March 11, but no later.

Thank you to everyone who submitted a contribution. I look forward to seeing you at the workshop.

Cheers,

Charles
